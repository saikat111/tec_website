TEXTILE ENGINEERING DEPARTMENT

TMSS Engineering College gladly welcomes students to the department of Textile Engineering. The historical fact is that textile industry led the industrialization of the economically strong and developed as well as developing countries of the world today. Bangladesh has gained advancement in the competition to capture a share of the global textile and apparel manufacturing industry during its migration from the developed countries. Today the textile and apparel industry is the most important industrial sector in Bangladesh Degree offered with duration: Four years program, B.Sc. (Textile Engineering), two semesters per year.     WHY CHOOSE TEXTILE ENGINEERING?

    Textile is the second basic requirement among the five requirements of human. To fulfill this large demand of the world populations with sustainable & hygienic textile products, textile and apparel industry in Bangladesh has been placing an increasing demand of smart, highly skilled manpower with technological and managerial background. Besides, there are huge demands for textile engineer in other field of social and economic activities.

    JOB PLACEMENT FACILITY:

    After completing four year B.Sc. in Textile Engineering program, a textile graduate can start his career with the following relevant areas:

    ✔About 5500 garments industry in Bangladesh.

    ✔About 750 spinning, weaving and Knitting, Dyeing and Finishing mills in Bangladesh..

    ✔Bangladesh Loom Board, Sericulture Institute, Various govt. and Non-govt. textile institutions (Universities, Colleges, diploma Institutes) and BCS Examination.
