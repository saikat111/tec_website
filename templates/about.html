  • Mission:

    TMSS Engineering College has a transformative impact on national and inter-national development in Engineering. To provides its best efforts to develop human resources in the field of Engineering, to meet the ever changing needs of the country and of the world by adopting specified academic curriculums and applying modern, scientific teaching and learning methods by implementing proper educational environment.

  • Vision:

    Our vision is to develop TEC with various offshoots and make it a world-class institution on Bangladeshi platform & will gain national and global recognition. Graduate students of TEC are contributing their acquired technical knowledge & skill effectively in the National and Global development.

  • OBJECTIVE

    With the increase of economic growth of the world the demand of skilled manpower is also increasing rapidly. Although there is a wide expansion of traditional system of education in our country, it can not face digital challenge of the 21st century. As a result demand for technical education is increasing day by day. So to provide excellent broad- based technical education to the students TMSS Engineering College committed to provide best quality learning experiences with comprehensive knowledge and skill based education necessary for the country and emphasis on their professional development in the field of Engineering.
